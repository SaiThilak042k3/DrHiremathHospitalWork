Key Relationships Summary

The entities within this ecosystem are interconnected. Agencies manage specific websites and utilize various digital platforms for marketing. Leads generated are routed through different mechanisms—some automated, some manual—to designated sales personnel. A key challenge and opportunity lie in streamlining these handoffs and centralizing communication channels, which is addressed in the proposed and future states.

Agency-Website: Xbrand → drvishwannathhiremath.com; HTA → drhiremathhospitals.in (marketing); AppAdd → Manages multiple sites including drhiremathhospitals.in.

Lead Flow (Current): Xbrand leads → Xbrand WhatsApp Automation → Gowri. HTA leads → MBG Cards WhatsApp Automation (no lead loss) → Asha.

Automation Integration (Current): MBG Cards handles all Meta (WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook) automation for HTA.

Automation Integration (Proposed): Integrate AppAdd's website leads with MBG Cards WhatsApp automation (clubbing AppAdd with MBG).

Centralization: Karthik's new channels (YouTube, Instagram, WhatsApp) to be integrated across automations.

Future Vision: Advanced Digital Engagement

This outlines a long-term vision for a highly sophisticated digital engagement strategy. It focuses on advanced automation through intelligent WhatsApp bots for different service lines and integration with Facebook Messenger, aiming for a seamless omnichannel patient journey from inquiry to appointment booking.

A. Sophisticated WhatsApp Automation Bots (Two Primary Bots)

Guiding patients from initial inquiry to appointment booking, reducing manual intervention.

1. Xbrand WhatsApp Bot (High-Ticket Dental)

Trigger: Ad Click (drvishwannathhiremath.com) / Direct WhatsApp
↓
Step 1: Welcome & Initial Qualification Show Details

"Hello [Patient Name], welcome... What brings you here?"

  • Opt 1: Interested in full mouth dental implants.
  • Opt 2: General dental query.
  • Opt 3: Book an appointment.
↓
Step 2: Information Gathering & Personalized Content Show Details

If Opt 1 (Implants): Previous consultations? Testimonials/Video? Links to YouTube, Website, Instagram. Offer free initial consultation?

If Opt 2 (General Query): Describe query. Links to FAQs.

If Opt 3 (Book Appointment): "Excellent! ... What's your preferred date/time range?" (Checks calendar).

↓
Step 3: Pre-Consultation Nurturing (for Implant Leads not booking immediately) Show Details

Automated follow-ups: success stories, doctor profiles, benefits. "Ready to book? [Booking Link]"

↓
Step 4: Appointment Booking & Confirmation Show Details

Patient selects date/time. Bot confirms, sends calendar invite, pre-appointment instructions. "Specialist will call to confirm."

↓
Step 5: Sales Handoff to Gowri (Complex Cases/Direct Booking)
↓
End: Appointment Booked / Lead Handed Off
