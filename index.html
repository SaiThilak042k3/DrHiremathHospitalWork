1. Xbrand WhatsApp Bot (Dedicated for High-Ticket Dental)

Trigger: Ad Click (drvishwannathhiremath.com) / Direct WhatsApp Message
↓
Step 1: Welcome & Initial Qualification
  • "Hello [Patient Name]..."
  • Options: "Interested in implants", "General query", "Book appointment"
↓
Patient Selects Option
If "Interested in implants"
↓
Step 2a: Info Gathering & Personalized Content
  • Previous consultations? (Yes/No/Unsure)
  • Testimonials/Video? (Yes/No)
  • Links to YouTube, Website, Instagram
  • Offer free initial consultation? (Yes/No)
↓
Patient Wants Consultation?
If Yes
↓
Step 4: Appointment Booking & Confirmation (Go to shared booking flow)
If No / Later
↓
Step 3: Pre-Consultation Nurturing (Automated follow-ups: success stories, doctor profiles, benefits, booking link)
↓
Ongoing Nurturing / Potential Future Booking
If "General query"
↓
Step 2b: General Query Handling
  • Briefly describe query
  • Provide links to general FAQs / Connect with specialist
↓
Query Resolved / Manual Handoff if complex
If "Book appointment"
↓
Step 2c: Direct Appointment Inquiry (Go to shared booking flow)
↓
Shared Appointment Booking Flow (for Xbrand)
  • "Preferred date/time range?"
  • Bot checks real-time calendar (CRM/Scheduling integration)
  • Patient selects date/time
  • Bot confirms appointment, sends calendar invite, pre-app instructions
↓
Step 5: Sales Handoff (for Complex Cases/Direct Booking) to Gowri (Sales Manager)
↓
Appointment Booked / Lead Handed Off
